The Dynamic Duo

We’re both called Alix/Alex, and we’re rarely ever apart, so when we moved over here from Ireland, the best way to differentiate between us was to adopt the title of ‘Lix and Lex’. We were forced together as young spuds by our parents, and the dynamic duo began. Neither of us can exactly remember what made us enjoy drawing, there was no epiphany, we were just lucky enough to have a natural talent, and being competitive children we wanted to be the best, so we practiced. So for us the art side of ESSR was a bonus when we joined, but for the most part, the opportunity to work with some of the best people we’ve ever met, who’ve really made us feel like a little ESSR family, whilst also helping others by raising awareness and fighting the stigma against mental health was obviously something most only dream of.

Hopefully when you see our designs, you’ll see the message we are conveyaing. We wanted all of the designs to be linked by a theme, so they’re all loosely based around you, your own health and spreading happiness and open-mindedness.

Check them out, support the cause with us and spread the word.

Lots of love, Lix and Lex xoxo
